About UPEC Federation

Upper East Coast Federation

Enduring to the finish line

Our Mission

To nurture and empower the youth of the Upper East Coast through spiritual development, leadership training, and community service.

Our Vision

To see every young person in the UPEC Federation grounded in faith, equipped for service, and ready for the coming of Christ.

AY Pledge

Loving the Lord Jesus, I promise to take an active part in the work of the Adventist Youth Ministry, doing what I can to help others and to finish the work of the gospel in all the world.

AY Aim

The Advent Message to all the world in my generation.

AY Motto

The love of Christ compels us.

2026 Goal

Build stronger relationships and establish clubs in Cane Grove

Our History

The Upper East Coast Federation was founded in 1982, uniting seven churches along the East Coast of Demerara to strengthen youth ministry in the region. For over 40 years, UPEC has been developing young leaders and spreading the gospel throughout our communities.
